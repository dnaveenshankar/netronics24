ABOUT NETRONICS

Netronics '24: Premier Symposium

Launched in 2023, Netronics has quickly become one of PSGCAS's largest symposiums, drawing over 1,000 participants annually. From its beginnings as Netronics '23 to the current Netronics '24, it reflects the growth and success of our department's flagship event.

Netronics '24 offers a mix of tech and non-tech challenges, including the popular 'Test Your Bond' event. The symposium culminates in an overall championship trophy, with certificates for all participants.
